## Primary Research Sources

- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent
- **Focus**: Pre-print research papers
- **Categories**: cs.AI, cs.LG, cs.CL, cs.CV
- **Update Frequency**: Daily
- **Use for**: Latest research, academic developments

- **URL**: https://paperswithcode.com

**Signal indicators for "truly trending" content:**
- Specific post/article with verifiable high engagement (views, reposts, replies)
- Named author + named trigger event
- Cross-platform spread (X → Reddit → Hacker News)
- Response posts or rebuttals from other researchers

## Content Freshness Guidelines

- **Breaking news**: Within 24 hours
- **Product releases**: Within 1 week
- **Research papers**: Within 1 month
- **Trend analysis**: Within 3 months
- **Historical context**: Any timeframe

## Quality Indicators

**Trusted indicators:**
- Official company announcements
- Peer-reviewed publications
- Direct quotes from researchers/executives
- Technical documentation
- Reproducible benchmarks

**Questionable indicators:**
- Anonymous sources
- Social media rumors
- Unverified leaks
- Marketing hype without substance
- Contradictory claims across sources
